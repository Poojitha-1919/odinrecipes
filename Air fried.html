Air Fryer Potstickers

    Ingredients

  • ½ pound ground pork
  • 1 (4 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and chopped
  • 1 (4 ounce) can shiitake mushrooms, drained and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce
  • 1 (12 ounce) package round dumpling wrappers

    Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Combine ground pork, water chestnuts, shiitake mushrooms, sesame oil, soy sauce, and Sriracha in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook until pork is no longer pink, about 6 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and let sit until cool enough to handle.
  5. Lay out 8 dumpling wrappers on a clean work surface.
  6. Place a heaping teaspoonful of pork mixture in the middle of each wrapper.
  7. Pull both sides up like a taco and pinch the tops until sealed.
  8. Cook in batches in the pre-heated air fryer for 3 minutes.
  9. Use tongs to flip the potstickers and cook 3 minutes more.
  10. Transfer to a paper-towel lined plate.
  11. Repeat with remaining dumpling wrappers and filling.
